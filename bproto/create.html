How to use

Click anywhere on the canvas to place down points. 2 points create one tree

You can draw as many trees as you want

Press C to clear canvas and start over

Click submit to save your sketch

Happy drawing!

How this works

You are creating fractals: a repeating pattern that you can customize

The first point determines where the plant starts growing from

The second point determines how big the plant will be and the angle it grows
