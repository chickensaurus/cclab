THE CABOODLE

Scientific Name:

Anguis multivermis

Discovered by / at:

Joint effort of UN, Year 5813479

Family:

Unable to fit into existing taxonomic structures. Looks to be the only life form on this plane.

Habitat:

It is believed that a rare astronomical event warped a star into this 2D plane.

Appearance:

The creature pulsates as it moves in a controlled linear motion, only capable of spreading across the wide barren land. Its color seems to change depending on which angle we look at it from.

Key Characteristics:

  • Looks shiny
  • Never crosses paths
  • Only makes right angled turns
  • Will conquer all available land

What we know so far about The Caboodle? The Caboodle thrives on expansion. It oozes across the surface, spreading and consuming anything it comes across. Its movements are designed to maximize the area it covers to extract all nutrients from the land. It is believed that the creature is capable of absorbing energy from the fallen star it inhabits. Only time will tell what happens to the creature once it consumes its entire plane of existance.
Guidelines for human interaction with this creature Do not approach. Interact through the probe by sending signals with keys or by directing the creature's attention with the cursor.
